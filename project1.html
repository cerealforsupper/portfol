Integrated Design Challenge (IDC)

This was a project where my team and I were tasked to design and build a robot system that could support disaster relief operations in post-disaster situations. The bot used systems like Arduino and Raspberry Pi to carry out the tasks.

The bot was to traverse a playfield and collect objects from it, and return it to the collection zone.

The Bot

The bot was made with a claw collection system, a computer vision trained model (Raspberry Pi and Camera), Bluetooth remote connection subsystem, and a line tracing feature.
