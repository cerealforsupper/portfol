ISS

In this project, my team and I undertook a comprehensive research initiative aimed at exploring the application and effectiveness of Peltier cells as a potential cooling system. Our objective was to analyze the thermoelectric properties of Peltier modules, evaluate their performance under various conditions, and assess their viability as an alternative to traditional cooling methods. The project involved both theoretical research and practical experimentation, allowing us to better understand the advantages, limitations, and real-world applicability of Peltier-based cooling technology.
