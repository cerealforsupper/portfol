Electronics Coursework

My task was to design and build an electronic muscle stimulator to improve blood circulation and muscle contraction by sending short electrical pulses to the body's nerves for 30 seconds. The frequency of the pulses was to be adjustable as well.

The two 555 timer ICs were connected through a 6V signal from the monostable 555 timer IC’s output to the astable 555 timer IC’s reset, allowing the astable 555 IC to stop producing frequencies after the monostable 555 finishes the 30s cycle.
